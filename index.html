About

I am currently a PhD student at GRAAL, Université Laval, QC, Canada with Jacques Corbeil, taking over from late François Laviolette, co-tutored by Cécile Capponi at QARMA, LIS, AMU, Marseille, France. I started my PhD in September 2017 and I am mainly interested in multi-view and statistical machine learning. My work has lead me to focus on ensemble methods, PAC-Bayesian learning, interpretable approaches and, lately causality. I mainly apply those models to biological data, with the goal of extracting new biomarkers

Proud developer and maintainer for the multi-learn GitHub organization, and active member of the Corbeil Lab machine learning experts team.
